Disclosure Policy

Transparency about how we earn revenue and maintain editorial independence

Our Commitment to Transparency

At NYC Money Guide, we believe transparency builds trust. This disclosure policy explains how we earn revenue, our affiliate relationships, and how we maintain editorial independence while providing free financial guidance to New Yorkers.

1. Why We Have a Disclosure Policy

As a financial guidance website, we have a responsibility to be transparent about any financial relationships that might influence our content. This policy:

  • Complies with FTC guidelines on endorsements and testimonials
  • Maintains reader trust through transparency
  • Ensures clear separation between editorial and commercial content
  • Provides full disclosure of any potential conflicts of interest

2. Our Revenue Model

NYC Money Guide is supported through several revenue streams that allow us to provide free content:

Display Advertising

We show relevant, non-intrusive ads from vetted advertising networks.

Affiliate Marketing

We earn commissions on products we genuinely recommend through affiliate links.

Sponsored Content

Occasional sponsored posts from trusted brands that align with our mission.

3. Affiliate Marketing Disclosure

3.1 What Are Affiliate Links?

Affiliate links are special URLs that track referrals. When you click an affiliate link and make a purchase, we may earn a commission at no additional cost to you.

3.2 How We Use Affiliate Links

  • We only recommend products and services we genuinely believe in
  • Commissions never influence our recommendations
  • We prioritize products that benefit NYC residents specifically
  • We disclose affiliate relationships clearly

3.3 Affiliate Programs We Participate In

We currently participate in affiliate programs with:

  • Financial Services: Bank account referrals, credit card offers, investment platforms
  • NYC Services: Local service providers, utility comparisons, transportation services
  • Product Recommendations: Tools and resources we personally use and recommend

Important Note

Our participation in affiliate programs does not affect the price you pay. You pay the same price whether you use our link or go directly to the vendor. Commissions are paid by the vendor, not by you.

4. Sponsored Content Policy

4.1 What is Sponsored Content?

Sponsored content is created in partnership with a brand or advertiser. We maintain strict guidelines:

  • All sponsored content is clearly labeled as "Sponsored" or "In Partnership With"
  • We only accept sponsorships from companies we believe provide value to our readers
  • We maintain editorial control over the content
  • Sponsored content must align with our mission and values

4.2 Disclosure Requirements

All sponsored content includes:

  • Clear "Sponsored" label at the top of the article
  • Disclosure within the first paragraph
  • Transparency about the nature of the partnership
  • No attempt to hide the sponsorship

5. Advertising Disclosure

5.1 Display Advertising

We use advertising networks to display relevant ads. These ads:

  • Are clearly distinguishable from editorial content
  • Are marked as "Advertisement" or "Ad"
  • Do not influence our editorial decisions
  • Are vetted for relevance to NYC residents

5.2 Native Advertising

Any native advertising (content designed to look like editorial) is:

  • Clearly labeled as "Sponsored Content" or "Advertisement"
  • Separated from editorial content
  • Subject to the same disclosure requirements as sponsored content

6. Editorial Independence

Our Editorial Promise

Revenue sources never influence our editorial content. We maintain a strict firewall between our business and editorial teams. Our recommendations are based on research, experience, and what's best for NYC residents—not commissions.

6.1 How We Maintain Independence

  • No Pay-for-Play: We never accept payment for positive reviews
  • Product Testing: We test products before recommending them
  • Transparent Reviews: We disclose both pros and cons of products
  • Reader-First Approach: Our readers' interests always come first

7. Example Disclosures

What You'll See on Our Site

Affiliate Link Disclosure: "This article contains affiliate links. If you make a purchase through these links, we may earn a commission at no extra cost to you."

Sponsored Content Disclosure: "This post is sponsored by [Company Name]. All opinions are our own."

Advertising Disclosure: "Advertisement" or "Ad" clearly displayed on all advertising units.

8. FTC Compliance

We adhere to Federal Trade Commission (FTC) guidelines for endorsements and testimonials, including:

  • Clear and conspicuous disclosure of material connections
  • Honest opinions and experiences
  • No misleading claims about products or services
  • Regular review of compliance with FTC guidelines

9. Your Responsibility as a Reader

As a reader, you should:

  • Always read disclosures before making purchases
  • Conduct your own research before financial decisions
  • Understand that our content is educational, not financial advice
  • Report any undisclosed commercial content to us

10. How to Identify Commercial Content

Look for these indicators:

  • Affiliate Links: Typically marked with asterisks (*) or disclaimers
  • Sponsored Content: Clearly labeled at the top of articles
  • Advertising: Marked as "Ad" or "Advertisement"
  • Product Recommendations: Often include disclosure statements

11. Changes to This Disclosure Policy

We may update this disclosure policy as our business model evolves. Significant changes will be announced on our website. The "Last Updated" date at the top of this page indicates when revisions were made.

12. Questions and Concerns

If you have questions about our disclosure practices or suspect a violation of this policy:

  • Email: disclosure@nycmoneyguide.com
  • Response Time: We aim to respond within 3-5 business days
  • Reporting: Please include specific URLs and details of your concern

Our Commitment to You

We're committed to providing valuable, honest financial guidance to NYC residents. Our disclosure practices are designed to maintain your trust while allowing us to continue offering free content. Thank you for being part of our community.

Final Reminder

NYC Money Guide provides educational content only. Always consult with qualified financial professionals before making financial decisions. Past performance does not guarantee future results, and all investments involve risk.
