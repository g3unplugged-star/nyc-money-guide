Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 2026

Summary: NYC Money Guide respects your privacy. We collect minimal data, never sell your information, and use cookies only to improve your experience. This policy explains how we handle your information when you use our website.

1. Information We Collect

We collect different types of information to provide and improve our services:

1.1 Information You Provide Directly

  • Contact Information: When you subscribe to our newsletter or contact us, we collect your name and email address.
  • User Content: Comments, feedback, or questions you submit through our contact forms.
  • Communication Data: Records of your correspondence with us.

1.2 Automatically Collected Information

  • Usage Data: Pages visited, time spent, referring websites, and browsing patterns.
  • Device Information: Browser type, operating system, device type, and IP address (anonymized).
  • Cookies & Tracking: We use cookies to enhance user experience and analyze website traffic.

1.3 Information from Third Parties

  • Analytics Providers: Anonymous data from Google Analytics about how users interact with our site.
  • Social Media Platforms: If you interact with us on social media, we may receive basic profile information.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • To Provide Services: Deliver the financial content and resources you request.
  • To Communicate: Send newsletters, updates, and respond to your inquiries.
  • To Improve Our Website: Analyze usage patterns to enhance content and user experience.
  • To Ensure Security: Protect our website and users from fraud and abuse.
  • To Comply with Laws: Meet legal obligations and enforce our terms.

3. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

3.1 Essential Cookies

Necessary for the website to function properly:

  • Session management
  • Security features
  • Load balancing

3.2 Analytics Cookies

Help us understand how visitors interact with our website:

  • Google Analytics (anonymous data only)
  • Page view tracking
  • Traffic source analysis

3.3 Advertising Cookies

Used by our advertising partners to display relevant ads:

  • Google AdSense
  • Affiliate marketing networks
  • Retargeting campaigns

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Note that disabling cookies may affect website functionality.

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. We may share information in these limited circumstances:

  • Service Providers: With trusted partners who help us operate our website (hosting, analytics, email services).
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law or to protect our rights and safety.
  • Business Transfers: In connection with a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets.
  • With Your Consent: When you explicitly agree to share your information.

5. Data Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your information:

  • Secure Socket Layer (SSL) encryption for data transmission
  • Regular security assessments
  • Access controls and authentication procedures
  • Regular software updates and patches

However, no internet transmission is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

6. Your Privacy Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights:

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal data.
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate data.
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your personal data.
  • Opt-Out: Unsubscribe from marketing communications.
  • Data Portability: Request transfer of your data to another service.
  • Restrict Processing: Request limitation of how we use your data.

To exercise these rights, contact us at privacy@nycmoneyguide.com.

7. Third-Party Links

Our website contains links to third-party websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these sites. We encourage you to review their privacy policies before providing any personal information.

8. Children's Privacy

Our website is not intended for children under 16. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe we have collected information from a child, please contact us immediately.

9. Data Retention

We retain your personal information only as long as necessary:

  • Newsletter Subscribers: Until you unsubscribe
  • Contact Inquiries: 3 years from last contact
  • Analytics Data: 26 months (Google Analytics standard)
  • Server Logs: 30 days

10. International Data Transfers

Our website is hosted in the United States. If you access our site from outside the US, your information may be transferred to and processed in the US, which may have different data protection laws than your country.

11. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy periodically. We will notify you of significant changes by posting the new policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date. We encourage you to review this policy regularly.
