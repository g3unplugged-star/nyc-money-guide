  1. Determine total amount for laddering (minimum $5,000 recommended)

NYC CD vs High-Yield Savings: When to Choose Each

Choose CDs when:

  • You have a specific NYC goal with known timeline (apartment purchase in 18 months)
  • You want to lock in rates before potential Fed decreases
  • You won't need the money before term ends (high certainty)
  • You can build a ladder for semi-liquid access
  • The CD rate is 0.25%+ higher than savings account

Choose HYSA when:

  • You need full liquidity (emergency fund core)
  • You're saving for multiple uncertain NYC goals
  • Rates are rising (don't lock in lower rates)
  • Your balance fluctuates frequently (NYC variable income)
  • You're building initial savings (under $5,000)

NYC-Specific FDIC Verification

Before depositing money with any institution, verify FDIC/NCUA status:

  5. Beware of "FDIC-insured" investments: Only deposits are insured, not investments
