How can I create a realistic NYC budget for 2026? +

Follow these steps for a realistic 2026 NYC budget:

  1. Download our budget template from the resources section above
  2. Start with net income (after NYC taxes, which are ~34% of gross)
  3. Use the 25-30% rule for rent (of net income, not gross)
  4. Factor in borough-specific costs using our tables above
  5. Include mandatory NYC expenses:
    • MTA: $147/month (projected 2026 fare)
    • Utilities: $180-300/month depending on building
    • NYC income tax: 3.876% of gross income
