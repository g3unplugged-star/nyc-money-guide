Common NYC Secured Card Mistakes to Avoid

NYC Secured Card Pitfalls: 1) Using too much of your limit (over 30% hurts scores), 2) Missing payments (30-day late stays on report 7 years), 3) Closing the card after graduation (hurts credit age), 4) Applying for multiple cards simultaneously (multiple hard inquiries), 5) Using for cash advances (high fees, no grace period), 6) Not monitoring credit reports for errors (common with new files), 7) Assuming all secured cards graduate (some don't—read terms carefully).

NYC-Specific Warning: Some NYC-targeted "credit repair" companies offer overpriced secured cards with high fees and poor terms. Always verify through official channels. Legitimate secured cards are offered directly by banks and credit unions, not through third-party "credit building" services charging setup fees.
