Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about contacting NYC Money Guide

Can you provide personalized financial advice for my specific NYC situation?

While we provide general educational content about NYC finances, we cannot provide personalized financial advice. For specific financial planning, we recommend consulting with a certified financial planner or advisor licensed in New York State. However, we can direct you to helpful resources and general strategies that apply to common NYC financial situations.

What types of partnerships do you offer for brands?

We offer several partnership opportunities including sponsored content, affiliate partnerships, display advertising, and collaborative guides. We only partner with brands that align with our mission of helping New Yorkers save money and improve their financial health. All partnerships are clearly disclosed to our readers. Visit our Partnerships page for more information.

How long does it take to get a response to my inquiry?

We aim to respond to all inquiries within 24-48 hours during business days (Monday-Friday). Partnership and media inquiries may take 3-5 business days as they often require review by multiple team members. If you haven't heard back within this timeframe, please check your spam folder or resend your message with "FOLLOW-UP" in the subject line.

Do you accept guest posts or content submissions?

We occasionally accept guest posts from financial experts, NYC professionals, and writers with unique perspectives on NYC living and finances. All submissions must meet our editorial standards and provide genuine value to our NYC-based audience. Please email your pitch to submissions@nycmoneyguide.com with writing samples and topic ideas.

Can I suggest a topic for a future article or guide?

Absolutely! We welcome topic suggestions from our readers. Many of our most popular articles started as reader suggestions. When suggesting topics, please be as specific as possible about the NYC financial challenge or question you'd like us to address. Include details about your borough, income range, or specific situation if relevant.
